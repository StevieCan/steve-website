Intro

Went to school at the Kelley School of Business from Indiana University. After about five years in sales, I realized that I wanted a change in profession. I enjoyed being in sales; however, it was a hectic lifestyle that required a lot of travel and time commitment including the weekends. I consider myself a religious person and like to attend Sunday services at my home church, Hebron Presbyterian Church. After much reflection and advice from friends that switched into IT, I researched the top coding bootcamps located in Chicago.

I committed to Actualize Coding Bootcamp after speaking with alumni and peers who graduated from other top schools including Northwestern and Fullstack. After three months of intensive schooling, I officially became a web developer; however, with the title came the most challenging obstacle to come. That desire to learn was instilled in me but it was up to me to continue to seek information and practice what I had learned. To this day, I am continuing to further my education by taking small classes and learning new languages. By the way, check out my awesome work.

Work

I am a firm believer in working hard to get what you want. I started working as an eight grader as a caddy at Twin Orchards Country Club. Working in manual labor did not limit me to value nutrition; however, it also taught me the basis of sales. I credit this job to building the foundation of my skills that helped me become so successful in sales. Being a caddy requires patience, great communication skills, leadership, and great relationships. I started as a caddy in 2003 and have maintained a healthy relationship with my boss even to this day. When they are short caddies, I gladly fill in for a couple weekends; however, I stopping going regularly in 2014.

Most recently worked for two years as a business development representative for Clean and Science. Headquartered in Seoul, South Korea, our main focus was to expand globally into the US market. Our sales team was in charge of all sales and operation within the U.S. and Canada. The two man Americas sales team consisted of myself and our sales manager. Collectively, we generated $1.5 million in revenue per annum.

About

- Born in Chicago, Illinois on June 14.
- Spent most of my childhood in the northwest suburbs in the town of Buffalo Grove.
- Graduated from Stevenson High School in Lincolnshire, IL.
- Attended Indiana University in Bloomington, IN

I grew up in a loving home and family was a value instilled in me at an early age. I also had a very active childhood that involved swimming lessons, park rec basketball, piano and violin lessons, flag football, and travel volleyball. I consider myself a very lucky kid that was able to experience a lot of things that I definitely took for granted at the time. These life experiences are something that shape the person I am today. I love to play sports and that shows through my dedication to Indiana University basketball and my beloved Chicago professional sports teams. Definitely a Cubs fan!
